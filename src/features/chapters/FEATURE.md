# chapters

Helps Elena understand the season she just lived through and return to what still matters without turning drift into failure.

## Surfaces in this folder

- `ChaptersScreen.tsx` - chapter inventory and entry point.
- `ChapterDetailScreen.tsx` - retrospective canvas for a season.
- `ChapterAlignScreen.tsx` - reconnects chapter meaning to life direction.
- `ChapterDigestSettingsScreen.tsx` - controls cadence and digest posture.
- `chapterSnippet.ts`, `chapterOpenSource.ts`, and read/dismissal helpers - lightweight ways to surface reflection.

## Notes

Chapters are sensemaking, not planning. The strongest product move is helping Elena notice meaning and return gently, not auto-prescribing a next project.
