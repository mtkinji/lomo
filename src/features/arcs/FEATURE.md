# arcs

Helps Sarah name who she is becoming and see ordinary action as evidence of that direction without reducing identity to a scorecard.

## Surfaces in this folder

- `ArcsScreen.tsx` - primary Arc inventory.
- `ArcCreationFlow.tsx` and `ArcDraftContinueFlow.tsx` - turn aspiration into an Arc.
- `ArcDetailScreen.tsx` - canvas for identity direction, linked Goals, and progress context.
- `GoalDetailScreen.tsx` - Goal canvas within the Arc context.
- `arcGuidance.ts`, `arcCreationSurvey.ts`, and `arcHeroLibrary.ts` - guidance and visual language for naming direction.

## Notes

Arcs carry identity, not productivity categories. Any progress surface here should help Sarah recognize becoming without making the Arc feel like a metric.
