# Sarah: See Who I'm Becoming

## Audience / Persona

Audience: `audience-faith-and-values-driven-builders`  
Persona: Sarah

Sarah wants her goals and actions to reflect who she is becoming across faith, family, work, service, and personal growth.

## Hero JTBD

`jtbd-see-who-im-becoming` - Help me see who I'm becoming so I act like that person today.

## Job Flow

1. Sense that a life direction matters.
2. Find words for that direction.
3. Turn the direction into an Arc.
4. Choose a goal that expresses the Arc.
5. Capture ordinary actions that belong to the Arc.
6. Notice progress without reducing it to metrics.
7. Reflect on the season and adjust.

## Current Kwilt Flow

1. FTUE starts from aspiration and Arc creation.
2. Arcs hold long-term identity direction.
3. Goals connect outcomes to Arcs.
4. Activities capture lived action.
5. Force Intent vs Actual can reveal mismatches.
6. Chapters can show a retrospective pattern.

## Offerings

- Arc generation and naming.
- Goal creation and Arc linkage.
- Activities and retroactive alignment.
- Forces as meaning dimensions.
- Chapters as retrospective evidence.

## Gaps

- Sarah needs more help seeing ordinary actions as evidence of becoming.
- Goal creation should more clearly test whether the goal expresses the Arc.
- Chapters should feed back into Arc confidence and next goals without becoming future planning.

## Aspirational Design Challenge

How might we help Sarah see ordinary faithful action as evidence of who she is becoming, without turning identity into a scorecard?
