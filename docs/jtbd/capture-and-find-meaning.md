## When this job is hot

The user's day fills with action — some intentional, some maintenance, some surprise. Most of it is forgotten by next week. They want a way to record what happened that's lighter than journaling, smarter than a task list, and that connects what they did back to who they're becoming — without forcing alignment in the moment of capture.

## What "done" feels like for the user

Capture is fast — the system never blocks them. Anchoring to an Arc/Goal is gentle and retroactive. Over time, more of their lived work participates in their story of becoming, and the unanchored work that's just maintenance is named as such, not as failure.
