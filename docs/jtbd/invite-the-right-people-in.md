## When this job is hot

Some growth happens alone, but a lot of it is supported (or sustained) by people — a spouse, a mentor, a small group, a coach. The user has people they'd want to share specific Goals or progress with, and others they categorically would not. Most apps treat sharing as a binary growth-loop trigger; the user wants it to feel like inviting someone into a particular room of their life, not posting to a feed.

## What "done" feels like for the user

The user can share a single Goal (or a curated slice) with a specific person without that opening the door to everything else. Invitations feel like extending trust, not like exposure. They never wonder "wait, who can see this?"
