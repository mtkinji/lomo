## When this job is hot

An AI answer becomes a proposed change to a to-do, Goal, Arc, plan, budget, or other durable part of the user's life system. The cost of a wrong write is higher than the cost of asking for a clear decision.

## What "done" feels like for the user

The user can see exactly what would change without being handed a second domain editor, correct or decline it conversationally, approve only the intended operation, and verify the authoritative result in the owning capability. Full editing stays in that capability; correction, revoke, and undo remain available wherever the domain can support them safely.
