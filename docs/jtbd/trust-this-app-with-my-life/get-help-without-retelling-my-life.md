## When this job is hot

The user has seen Kwilt help them do something and now asks naturally from a Goal, to-do, Chapter, or broader life situation. The question may be general, personally contextual, or actionable; they should not have to learn Kwilt's internal boundary first. They expect the system to understand visible context and relevant history when it matters, but they do not want hidden context to anchor or overexpose an ordinary question.

## What "done" feels like for the user

Kwilt answers directly, uses bounded personal context, invokes a capability, or recommends a better destination according to the actual question. It makes any active scope legible, retrieves only what is useful, and never forces a general question into a Kwilt object or action. The user can remove, add, or broaden context and can resume later without wondering what the conversation still assumes.
