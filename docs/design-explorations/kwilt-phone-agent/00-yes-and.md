# Yes-And: kwilt-phone-agent

## Original idea (restated)

Make Kwilt Keep an SMS / phone-number accessible agent service that helps users with their Kwilt JTBDs.

## Yes, and what if it could...

**Yes, and what if it could...** become the owned phone-number front door to the Kwilt life system.

- Serves: `jtbd-trust-this-app-with-my-life`
- Job elevation: the user does not need to choose the right app surface before asking Kwilt for help; the phone number becomes a reliable, permissioned door into Arcs, Goals, Activities, and Chapters.
- New value: Kwilt has its own channel instead of depending only on mobile, desktop, or Claude/ChatGPT.
- Cost delta vs. original: medium
- Anti-pattern check: pass if phone input is governed by the same permission, preview, and audit contract as other agent surfaces.

**Yes, and what if it could...** preserve the relational follow-through magic from the original Keep wedge.

- Serves: `jtbd-carry-intentions-into-action`
- Job elevation: birthdays, apologies, support moments, and drift reminders become carried threads, not forgotten good intentions.
- New value: the first paid WOW is emotionally obvious: Kwilt helped the user show up for someone.
- Cost delta vs. original: low
- Anti-pattern check: pass if drafts stay user-sent and the product avoids CRM, surveillance, or default-public sharing.

**Yes, and what if it could...** let users call when typing is too slow or the situation is messy.

- Serves: `jtbd-capture-and-find-meaning`
- Job elevation: voice capture handles driving, walking, emotionally loaded context, and long messy intention capture better than a text field.
- New value: a phone call can turn a rambling thought into a few concrete Activities or drafts.
- Cost delta vs. original: high
- Anti-pattern check: pass if voice summaries are confirmed, transcripts are minimized, and writes remain explicit.

**Yes, and what if it could...** make Kwilt queryable from a phone number.

- Serves: `jtbd-see-my-arcs-in-everyday-moments`
- Job elevation: the user can ask, "What did I say I wanted to do this weekend?" or "What do I know about Lily?" without opening the app.
- New value: Kwilt becomes present in everyday moments while the app remains the durable canvas.
- Cost delta vs. original: medium
- Anti-pattern check: pass if answers reveal only the requesting user's scoped data and avoid over-interpreting identity.

**Yes, and what if it could...** create the action substrate shared by Text Coach, MCP, desktop AI, and Weekly Options.

- Serves: `jtbd-trust-this-app-with-my-life`
- Job elevation: every agent surface obeys the same rules for capture, suggestion, confirmation, audit, and undo.
- New value: Kwilt can grow agent capability without each channel inventing its own mutation model.
- Cost delta vs. original: high
- Anti-pattern check: pass if permissions are plain-language and reversible.

**Yes, and what if it could...** become a Pro pillar around follow-through, not "more AI."

- Serves: `jtbd-move-the-few-things-that-matter`
- Job elevation: the paid value is Kwilt carrying important threads forward across time, not raw message volume or token usage.
- New value: pricing can be grounded in outcomes users feel: remembered intentions, useful prompts, drafts, and closed loops.
- Cost delta vs. original: low
- Anti-pattern check: pass if free capture remains generous and paid gating never blocks capture.

## Candidate-missing anchors

None. The earlier Text Coach exploration already added `jtbd-carry-intentions-into-action`, which is the missing demand-side home this idea needed.

## Frame recommendation

**Run design-thinking-loop with an expanded frame**: not "SMS coach" as a standalone product, but "Kwilt Phone Agent / Kwilt Keep as the owned phone-number surface for trusted capture, recall, planning, and follow-through."
