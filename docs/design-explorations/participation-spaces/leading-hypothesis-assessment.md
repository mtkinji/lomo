# Leading-Hypothesis Coverage Assessment

This document holds the leading **named Spaces + scoped participation/grants + person-centered projections** hypothesis against every cataloged use case. Scores are design-coverage estimates, not shipped delivery evidence.

After the lifecycle, ownership, safety, sponsorship, and scenario contracts were added, the hypothesis is eligible to converge as a **platform direction**. It has no hard failure, no use case remains at score 1, every family is at least coherently served, and every critical case in the convergence threshold scores 3.

This does not mean every capability is ready to build. It means the common model can now contain the known hard cases without distorting personal Kwilt or requiring a different people architecture.

## What the assessment teaches us

1. **The basic shape is elegant for normal-day participation.** It performs strongly for solo continuity, one household, assignment, and one-off sharing.
2. **The hard lifecycle stories strengthen separation.** Space ownership, authorship, responsibility, access, and payment must remain distinct; a clever generic role cannot replace them.
3. **Extended-family value uses a ladder.** One-off copy is enough for many cases; live sharing and a durable Space are earned only as collaboration becomes repeated.
4. **Support is a scoped relationship, not another Space.** This repairs the semantic gap between Goal co-ownership and accountability without creating a graph people must manage.
5. **Payment remains downstream of participation.** The source-ledger model contains sponsor loss and transfer without contaminating data authority.

## Convergence boundary

The critical walkthroughs now close source/ownership, grants, personal projections, offline truth, removal, and payment aftermath. The common platform model can converge.

Convergence does **not** authorize immediate implementation of family memories, adversarial co-parenting, dependent independence, adult-supported Screen Time, or high-risk abuse recovery. Each remains a capability or safety brief with its own evidence gate. They no longer require a competing system model.
