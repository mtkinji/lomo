# Faith- and values-driven builders

## Representative persona: Sarah

Sarah is trying to live coherently across work, family, faith, friendships, service, and personal growth. She is not trying to optimize every hour. She wants her commitments to reflect who she is becoming and what she is responsible for.

## Why Kwilt Might Matter

Kwilt can help Sarah name the big directions of her life without making them feel like projects. It can help her choose goals that express those directions and notice ordinary actions that belong to them.

## What Sarah Is Trying To Do

- Name the life directions that matter.
- Keep those directions present during ordinary weeks.
- Choose goals that fit her values instead of just her ambition.
- See faithful movement without turning it into a score.

## What Would Make This Feel Wrong

- Corporate productivity language.
- Treating faith, family, or character like KPIs.
- Overconfident AI interpretation of her inner life.
- Progress mechanics that create guilt.
